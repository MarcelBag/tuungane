IT Consultancy & Digital Solutions

  • Digital Transformation Strategies: Helping businesses modernize and innovate.
  • Internet & Infrastructure Consulting: Advising on connectivity, platforms, and tools.
  • Training & Capacity Building: Empowering local teams to adopt new technologies.

Legal and judicial advice and assistance

  • Providing free legal advice to vulnerable populations (women, children, displaced persons).
  • Judicial support: Accompanying victims of violence through legal processes.
  • Community mediation: Promoting peaceful resolution of minor conflicts.
  • Awareness & training: Educating communities on rights and access to justice.

Afya Digital Health for All

  • Mobile health education: SMS and WhatsApp tips for families.
  • Digital clinics: Teleconsultation to reach rural and underserved patients.
  • Maternal & child health: Community programs and digital reminders.

Sustainable Agriculture & Farmer Support

  • Training farmers in modern and climate-resilient techniques.
  • Digitally connecting farmers with local and global buyers.
  • Promoting eco-friendly and sustainable farming practices.
