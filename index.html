About Masomo

Masomo is our custom-built, modern, interactive school management system developed using Django. It provides comprehensive student, staff, and fee management functionalities with a clean, responsive dashboard. It integrates academic records, admissions, finance, and reporting modules to empower schools with a seamless digital management experience.
