Our story

Born in Bukavu. Built for Congo.

Tuunganes is a grassroots initiative using technology, innovation, and compassion to create community-led change especially for those affected by war, poverty, and marginalization in Bukavu and Goma.

We believe technology is more than tools it is a bridge to opportunity, education, and dignity.
