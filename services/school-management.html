Why a School Management System matters in Congo

Education is the backbone of every community yet in many parts of the DRC, especially in the East, schools are still mostly non-digital. Records live on paper, fee payments are tracked manually, and communication between teachers, parents, and administrators is slow or unreliable.

Masomo by Tuunganes modernizes how schools operate with a secure, multi-tenancy platform designed for Congo’s realities reliable offline-first workflows, clear finance tracking, and simple, mobile-friendly tools.

Pricing FAQs

What happens at the end of my trial?

You can choose a plan and your data remains intact. If you do nothing, your account pauses until you pick a plan.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes annual billing saves 20%. For multi-school deployments, contact us.

What are the payment options?

We support major cards and mobile money partners in DRC where available.

Can I change plans after subscribing?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime; prorated billing applies.

Is location tracking optional?

Yes. It’s opt-in, visible only to authorized parents/guardians, and respects consent.
