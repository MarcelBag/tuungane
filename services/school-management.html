Masomo School Management

Helping schools in Congo modernize education with digital tools for students, teachers and parents.

Education is the backbone of every community — but in many parts of the Democratic Republic of Congo, schools are still largely non-digital. Records are stored on paper, payments are tracked manually, and communication is often slow or unreliable.

At Tuunganes, we are changing this by building a modern, multi-tenancy School Management System that transforms how schools operate.

Why It Matters

For Students

Digitization keeps their grades, attendance, and progress always updated and motivates them to stay engaged.

For Teachers

The platform reduces administrative burden, automates report cards, and allows teachers to focus on teaching.

For Parents

Parents gain real-time access to their child’s academic performance, attendance, and even location, helping them stay involved.

For Administrators

The system ensures transparency in school finances, fees, and performance metrics, making management data-driven and efficient.

Key Features

  • Student profiles from enrollment to graduation
  • Class and timetable management
  • Automatic grade entry and report cards
  • Subscription and fee tracking
  • Multi-school support with secure data isolation

Safety and Security: Our Top Priority

Child safety is a major concern in Eastern Congo. Our system integrates protective features to keep students safe.

  • Embedded map and real-time location tracking for parents
  • Access control ensures only authorized parents can view data
  • Alerts and notifications when children arrive or leave school

By combining education with security and transparency, we are creating a safer learning environment for the next generation.
