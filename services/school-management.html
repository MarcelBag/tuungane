Why a School Management System matters in Congo

Education is the backbone of every community — yet in many parts of the DRC, especially in the East, schools are still mostly non-digital. Records live on paper, fee payments are tracked manually, and communication between teachers, parents, and administrators is slow or unreliable.

Masomo by Tuunganes modernizes how schools operate with a secure, multi-tenancy platform designed for Congo’s realities — reliable offline-first workflows, clear finance tracking, and simple, mobile-friendly tools.

Safety & Security: Our top priority

In Eastern DRC, child safety is a major concern. Masomo integrates protective features to keep students safe while respecting privacy and consent.

  • Embedded map and optional real-time location for authorized parents only
  • Granular access control and audit logs
  • Smart alerts when a child arrives at or leaves school
