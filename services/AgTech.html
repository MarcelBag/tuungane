The challenge

Eastern DRC needs
food security.

Agriculture is the backbone of communities in the DRC, yet many smallholder farmers face low productivity, lack of access to markets, and outdated techniques. Conflict and displacement have further disrupted food systems, leaving families vulnerable to hunger and malnutrition.

Tuunganes responds with practical programs: climate-smart training, simple digital tools for planning and market access, and community food initiatives designed around Congo's realities.
