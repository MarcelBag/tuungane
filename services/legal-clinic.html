Legal and Judicial Advice and Assistance

The Tuunganes Legal Clinic provides free legal and judicial assistance to vulnerable communities including women, children and displaced persons.

Our Mission

We aim to protect the rights of the most vulnerable by offering free legal advice, judicial assistance, community mediation and training. Our clinic is a safe space where victims of violence and marginalized groups can find support and justice.

Our Services

  • Free legal advice for women, children and displaced persons.
  • Judicial support to accompany victims of violence through legal procedures.
  • Community mediation to promote peaceful resolution of minor conflicts.
  • Awareness and training to educate communities on their rights and access to justice.

Contact the Legal Clinic

If you or someone you know needs legal support, please reach out to our Tuunganes Legal Clinic team.
