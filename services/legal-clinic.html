Legal and Judicial Assistance

Defending rights of vulnerable groups in Eastern DRC through free legal advice, judicial support and mediation.

Why It Matters in Congo

Armed conflicts and displacement in North and South Kivu have left women, children and entire communities exposed to violence and exploitation.

Our Clinic Provides

  • Free legal advice for marginalized groups.
  • Judicial support for victims of violence.
  • Community mediation to resolve conflicts peacefully.
  • Awareness and training on rights and access to justice.
