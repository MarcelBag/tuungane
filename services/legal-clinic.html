The challenge

Justice for
every person.

Communities in North and South Kivu face conflict, displacement, and exploitation daily. Many victims cannot afford a lawyer, don't know their rights, or fear retaliation. Cases involving children are too often ignored or buried in bureaucracy.

Tuunganes' Legal Clinic combines legal aid, protection services, and community rights education restoring dignity and opening real pathways to justice for those who need it most.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is the service really free?
+−

Yes. Basic legal aid and mediation are free. If a licensed lawyer must file a formal case, we always seek pro bono options first.

Do you go to court?
+−

We partner with licensed lawyers when needed. Many issues resolve through mediation, which is often faster and safer.

Is my information confidential?
+−

Absolutely. We obtain consent before every step, and nothing is ever shared without your knowledge.

How quickly can you help?
+−

Emergencies are prioritized the same day. Other cases are scheduled within 1–3 days.

Do you work outside Bukavu & Goma?
+−

Yes. We support cases across the DRC remotely and deploy on-site with local partners when necessary.
