Afya Digital Health for All

Healthcare should be accessible and data-driven. Afya connects rural families to clinicians through telehealth, mobile education and offline-first tools designed for low-connectivity environments.

  • Community health surveys via KoboToolbox
  • Data analysis for malaria prevention & health campaigns
  • AI-assisted decision support for early disease detection
  • Telehealth & mobile education for rural communities
