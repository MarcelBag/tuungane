Afya Digital Health for All

Connecting families in rural Congo to clinicians through telehealth, mobile education and digital health tools.

Why It Matters in Congo

Many families walk hours to reach basic health services. Preventable diseases like malaria and malnutrition remain widespread.

Our Health Tools

  • Teleconsultations for rural and underserved patients.
  • Mobile health education via SMS and WhatsApp.
  • Maternal and child health programs with digital reminders.
  • AI-assisted data collection to predict outbreaks early.
