The challenge

Eastern DRC
needs better health.

In Eastern DRC, many families walk hours for basic care. Preventable diseases like malaria and malnutrition remain common, and health information is often hard to access in remote and conflict-affected areas.

Afya blends teleconsultations, community-based data collection, and mobile education to bring timely advice and follow-up care to the last mile ‚Äî even without a stable internet connection.

The process

How Afya
works.

1

Community intake

A community health worker screens and records symptoms offline. Severe cases are flagged immediately.

2

Clinician review

A nurse or doctor reviews the case and contacts the family or CHW directly by phone or message.

3

Guidance & referral

Care advice is given remotely. Urgent cases are referred to the nearest facility with a pre-filled referral slip.

4

Follow-up & education

Automated reminders keep the family on track with treatment, vaccinations, and follow-up visits.
