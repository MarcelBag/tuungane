  1. Open Jira Cloud.

2. Selecting Your Time Range

Choose your perspective:

  • Weekly Your weekly rhythm and balance
  • Monthly Deep insights into trends
  • Yearly A complete story of your year

Each selection generates a unique narrative and visual style.

3. Generate Your Wrapped

  2. Click Generate Wrapped
  3. Explore your insights, messages, and activity trends
