About Us

Tuunganes is a dynamic organization committed to advancing digital solutions for schools, communities, and health services in Africa. We specialize in developing school management systems, public health tech platforms, IT support, and digital transformation services tailored for local communities.

Born from the need to bridge digital gaps in education and healthcare, Tuunganes envisions a connected, empowered, and tech-literate future for all.
