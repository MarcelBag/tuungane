About Tuunganes

We are a grassroots movement using technology, education and compassion to empower communities in Bukavu, Goma and beyond.

Who we are

Tuunganes is a dynamic organization committed to advancing digital solutions for schools, communities and health services in Africa.

Born in Bukavu (Eastern DRC), we envision a connected, empowered, tech-literate future for all.

Our Mission

Create meaningful, community-led change through technology, innovation, education and compassion.

Our Vision

A connected Great Lakes region where youth, women and families thrive with equal digital opportunities.

Our Values

  • Community first
  • Transparency & accountability
  • Learning mindset
  • Dignity & compassion
