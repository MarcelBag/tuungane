About Tuunganes

A community-driven initiative using technology, innovation, and social action to transform lives across the DRC and Africa.

Who we are

Tuunganes is dedicated to using technology, innovation, and social action to transform lives in the Democratic Republic of Congo starting from Bukavu and Goma and across Africa.

We believe technology is more than tools; it is a bridge to opportunity, education, and dignity…

Our Mission

To empower communities through technology, education, and health innovation bridging the digital divide and creating opportunities for all.

Our Vision

  • Every child has access to quality education powered by digital tools.
  • Communities use data and technology to fight disease and improve health outcomes.
  • Farmers and local entrepreneurs are connected to markets and resources.
  • Youth are trained in digital skills, preparing them for the future of work.
  • Technology drives peace, security, and development, even in conflict-affected regions.

What We Do

IT Consultancy & Digital Transformation

Advocacy & Social Programs

Promoting education, peace, and digital literacy.

Aid & Donations

Refurbishing donated devices to equip schools and labs.

Why Tuunganes Matters

Eastern DRC faces unique challenges… Tuunganes combines digital innovation with community-driven solutions.

Our initiatives are designed to:

  • Make education transparent, secure, and inclusive.
  • Strengthen healthcare systems using data-driven approaches.
  • Provide hope and tools for families affected by war and poverty.
  • Build a generation of skilled youth ready to lead Africa into the digital future.
