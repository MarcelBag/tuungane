Who we are

Born in Bukavu.
Built for Congo.

Tuunganes is dedicated to using technology, innovation, and social action to transform lives in the Democratic Republic of Congo starting from Bukavu and Goma and across Africa.

We help schools, communities, and organizations embrace digital transformation, build resilience, and improve quality of life even in some of the world's most challenging environments.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Bridging the digital divide.

To empower communities through technology, education, and health innovation creating opportunities for all, especially those left behind by conflict, poverty, and marginalization in Eastern DRC and across Africa.

Vision

An Africa where no one is left behind.

  • Every child has access to digital education.
  • Communities use data to fight disease and poverty.
  • Farmers and entrepreneurs are connected to markets.
  • Technology drives peace, even in conflict zones.
