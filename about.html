About Us

Tuunganes is a dynamic organization committed to advancing digital solutions for schools, communities, and health services in Africa. We specialize in developing school management systems, public health tech platforms, IT support, and digital transformation services tailored for local communities.

Born from the need to bridge digital gaps in education and healthcare, Tuunganes envisions a connected, empowered, and tech-literate future for all.

Uniting communities. Driving digital transformation. Empowering lives.

Tuunganes is a grassroots-driven initiative born in Bukavu, Eastern Democratic Republic of Congo. Our mission is to create meaningful, community-led change through the power of technology, innovation, education, and compassion. We are more than a nonprofit—we are a movement built by and for the people, especially those affected by war, poverty, and marginalization in regions like Bukavu and Goma.
